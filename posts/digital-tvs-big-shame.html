Digital TV’s big shame

2007-01-29 • TV • Comments

For some while now I’ve worked on the software side of digital TV. I used to be proud to be able to go into people’s homes and tell them that I helped write the software running on their FreeView receivers. Now, though, I’m more ashamed to be in any way associated with the trash that’s being broadcast. Who’s to blame? The providers, the participants, the media, the public?
