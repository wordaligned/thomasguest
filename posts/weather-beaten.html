Weather beaten

2008-08-07 • Shellfish, Drawing, Cornwall • Comments

We’re back from holiday a few days earlier than planned. Even if you’re camping in a field surrounded by sand-dunes and just a few hundred metres from the best beach in the world, the rain gets to you after a while.

Good:

  • body-boarding, rock-pooling, fighting the waves
  • watching Isobel and Alex ride their bicycles around the campsite
  • reading
  • a memorable meal in St Ives
  • the Barbara Hepworth sculpture garden
  • clear roads, for once

Not so good:

  • gusting wind
  • ceaseless rain
  • damp shoes
  • pulled muscle
  • car trouble
