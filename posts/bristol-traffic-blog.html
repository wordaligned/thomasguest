Bristol traffic blog

2008-08-25 • Bristol, Blog, Traffic • Comments

I hesitate to recommend another blog — just two weeks after I promoted Dave Moulton’s Bike Blog the author seems to be packing it in — but if you need to get from A to B in Bristol, subscribe to the Bristol Traffic blog:

In which we explore the options for getting round Bristol, and the consequences of those decisions.

Actually, it’s just a collection of pictures of people parking badly, and a sarcastic commentary. What’s not to like?
