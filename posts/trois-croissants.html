Trois croissants!

2009-03-16 • Cycling, Hoy, Breakfast, Alex • Comments

So who persuaded the mighty Sir Chris Hoy to appear in this silly advert?

  1. Poking fun at the French isn’t smart.
  2. Mocking their cycling decline is like kicking someone who’s already down.
  3. Bran flakes are sugar and chaff — hardly suitable for someone who burns lots of energy.
  4. Croissants are delicious. Maybe not something for every day. But delicious!

Here’s a proper breakfast food. One I’d be happy to see the Scottish sprint knight advertising.

Like Sir Chris, Alex needs real fuel. This morning he ate three croissants.
