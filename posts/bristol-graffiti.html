Bristol graffiti

2008-12-12 • Bristol, graffiti • Comments

Dunno how I found the Bristol Graffiti blog, but it’s a great place to view street art without actually having to venture outside. Here’s a gem posted today.

Zoom in and you can see 3dom and voyder have signed their work with links to myspace accounts. Also happening on the streets in Bristol: here’s a recent shot by award winning photographer Burnsy of some work by pavement artist Tom Guest (no relation).

Update, 2008-12-13. Just to prove I do get out!

As the Bristol Graffiti blog says:

… this blog isn’t here so people can live life through the web, it’s here to encourage you to get out and see stuff for yourself, so get down there!

Really, try and get to see the show.
